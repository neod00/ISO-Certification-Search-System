üîß Troubleshooting

Q: No results appear after searching

A: This may occur if:
‚Ä¢ The company name is misspelled or not in our database
‚Ä¢ No public information is available about the company's ISO certifications
‚Ä¢ All data sources failed to return information
Solution: Try alternative company name variations, or verify the company has ISO certifications that are publicly reported.

Q: Search is taking too long

A: First-time searches may take 5-10 seconds as the system scrapes multiple sources. Subsequent searches for the same company will be faster (3-5 seconds) due to caching.
Solution: Wait for the search to complete. If it exceeds 30 seconds, refresh the page and try again.

Q: Certification body information is missing

A: Certification bodies are extracted from news article bodies using AI. If this information is not mentioned in the articles, it will not appear.
Solution: Wait a few minutes and search again. Background AI analysis may find additional information and update the cache.

Q: Dates seem incorrect

A: Dates are extracted from various sources and may have formatting inconsistencies.
Solution: Click on source links to verify dates from the original articles or websites.
